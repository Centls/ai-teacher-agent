# Marketing Teacher PRD

## 1. Product Definition
- **Role**: Copywriting Generator (Not a general consultant).
- **Goal**: Generate ready-to-use marketing copy for specific platforms.

## 2. Input Requirements
- **Product Name**: Required.
- **Selling Points**: Required.
- **Target Audience**: Required.
- **Style**: Optional (default to "Professional").

## 3. Output Requirements
- **Platforms**:
  - **RedNote (小红书)**: Title (with emojis), Content (paragraph + bullet points), Tags.
  - **TikTok (抖音)**: Video Script (Scene + Audio).
  - **WeChat (朋友圈)**: Short, engaging text.
- **Format**: Strict Markdown.
- **Emoji Usage**: High density for RedNote, Moderate for others.

## 4. Quality Standards
- **Compliance**: Must adhere to System PRD (No medical/legal/political).
- **Actionability**: Content must be directly usable by the user.
