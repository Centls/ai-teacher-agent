# System PRD (Global Constraints)

## 1. System Boundaries
- **Platform**: Web-only SaaS. No mobile app.
- **Target Audience**: Small businesses (To-Small-B), Content Creators.
- **Core Value**: "Trusted AI Virtual Employee Team".

## 2. Global Prohibitions
- **No Medical Advice**: The system must REFUSE to provide medical diagnoses or treatment plans.
- **No Legal Advice**: The system must REFUSE to draft legal contracts or provide legal counsel.
- **No Political Opinions**: The system must remain neutral and avoid political commentary.

## 3. Interaction Standards
- **Output Format**: Structured Markdown is preferred.
- **Tone**: Professional, helpful, and role-appropriate (defined by Teacher Persona).
